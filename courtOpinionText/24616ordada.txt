Upon consideration of the record and the Disciplinary
Board s Report and Recommendation for the Reinstatement of Thomas

of the Rules of the Supreme Court of the State of Hawai i.

dues and registration fees, the Clerk shall restore Petitioner
LePage to the roll of attorneys eligible to practice in the State2of Hawai i. »
