ORDER AFFIRMING JUDGMENT ON APPEAL
(By:  Moon, C.J., Nakayama, and Recktenwald, JJ.;
Acoba, J., dissenting, with whom Duffy, J. joins)
Petitioner/defendant-appellee Randall Strong, Jr.
timely filed a petition for writ of certiorari on February 11,
2010, seeking review of the judgment of the Intermediate Court of
Appeals (ICA), filed on January 25, 2010, pursuant to its
November 25, 2009 published opinion in the above-entitled matter. 
Therein, the ICA vacated the April 7, 2008 “Order Granting in
Part and Denying in Part [Petitioner’s] Motion to Suppress
Evidence and Statements, Findings of Fact, and Conclusions of
Law” of the Circuit Court of the First Circuit and remanded the
matter to the trial court for further proceedings.  This court
accepted certiorari on March 23, 2010 and heard oral argument on
May 6, 2010.-2-Upon careful review of the record and the papers
submitted by the parties and having given due consideration to
the arguments advanced and the issues raised, including the
parties’ respective oral arguments,
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the ICA’s January 25, 2010
judgment on appeal is affirmed.
DATED:  Honolulu, Hawai# i, June 9, 2010.
