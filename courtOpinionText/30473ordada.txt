(By: Moon, C.J., Nakayama, Acoba, Duffy, and Recktenwald, JJ.)
Upon consideration of the petition filed in the supreme
court by petitioner Jeanmarc Offergeld, it appears that we lack
jurisdiction to conduct a de novo review of petitioner’s
grievances.  See HRS § 602-5 (Supp. 2009).  Therefore,
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the petition is dismissed.
