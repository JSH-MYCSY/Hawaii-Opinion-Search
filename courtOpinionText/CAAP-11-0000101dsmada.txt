Upon consideration of the August 1, 2011 letter from 
counsel for Defendant-Appellant, Marty Martins, informing this 
court that Mr. Martins had passed away during the pendency of this appeal and that counsel was aware of no person to properly serve as a substitute party, the December 30, 2011 Suggestion of Death Upon the Record Under HRAP Rule 43(a), the Plantiff-Appellee, State of Hawai' i's failure to move for a substitution, 
and the record and file herein, 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that this appeal is dismissed.
 DATED: Honolulu, Hawai' i, January 17, 2012.
