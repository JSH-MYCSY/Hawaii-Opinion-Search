Judgment filed by Defendant-Appellant Sylvia Cabral pro se on
 
September 26, 2014, the papers in support, and the records and

 September 25, 2014 and there is no authority for this court to
 reconsider a judgment.

Reconsideration of Judgment is dismissed.
