Upon review of the record, it appears that: 
(1)
 On November 1, 2011, Defendant/Cross-Claim
 
Defendant/Appellant Kathleen Patricia Morris (Appellant) filed a
 notice of appeal;
 
(2) The record on appeal was filed on April 29, 2013,
 
and the appellate clerk informed Appellant that the opening brief
 was due by June 10, 2013;
 
(3) On June 10, 2013, the appellate clerk granted
 
Appellant an extension of time for the opening brief to July 10,
 2013;

(4) On July 8, 2013, Appellant filed a notice of

Darren G. Russell for Paul J. Sulla;
 
(5) Appellant did not file the opening brief, or
 
request an extension of time, by the July 10, 2013 due date;
 
(6) On August 1, 2013, the appellate clerk informed 
Appellant that the time for filing the opening brief had expired and, pursuant to Rule 30 of the Hawai' i Rules of Appellate 
Procedure, the matter would be called to the court’s attention on August 12, 2013, for such action as the court deems proper, which could include dismissal; 
(7) The August 1, 2013 default notice was
 
electronically served on Appellant’s counsel, Darren G. Russell;
 and 
(8) Thereafter, Appellant did not file the opening
 
brief, or respond to the notice of default. 	Therefore, 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the appeal is dismissed.
