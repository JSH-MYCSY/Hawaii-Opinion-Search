APPEAL FROM THE CIRCUIT COURT OF THE FIRST CIRCUIT
 
(PROBATE NO. 10-1-0254)
 
ORDER DISMISSING APPEAL PURSUANT TO HRAP RULE 11(c)(2)
(By: Nakamura, C.J., Foley and Leonard, JJ.)
 
Upon review of the record, it appears that: 
(1)
 on July 30, 2012, Plaintiff-Appellant Barbara Ellen Sherrill
 
(Appellant) filed a notice of appeal; (2) on August 16, 2012,
 
Appellant's motion to proceed in forma pauperis was denied; 
(3) on August 29, 2012, Appellant was granted an extension to pay
 the filing until September 27, 2012; (4) on October 18, 2012 the
 appellate clerk informed Appellant that a filing fee of $300 was
 due, that the filing fee must be paid by October 28, 2012, and
 that failure to timely pay the filing fee would be brought to the
 attention of the court for such action as the court deemed
 appropriate, including dismissal of the appeal; and (5) thereafter,
 Appellant did not pay the filing fee. 
NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the appeal is
 
dismissed, pursuant to HRAP Rule 11(c)(2).
