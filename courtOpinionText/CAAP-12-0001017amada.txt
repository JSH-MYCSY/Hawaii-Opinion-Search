The Opinion of the court, filed on January 29, 2016,
 
is hereby corrected as follows:
 
1. On page 3, in the ninth line of the first full
 
paragraph, the word "and" should be inserted between "Rosa;" and
 
"that" so that as corrected, the text reads: ". . . believe
 Officer Rosa; and that Officer Rosa . . . ."
 
2. On page 9, in the fourth line of the second full
 
paragraph, the comma between "arrested" and "before" should be
 deleted so that as corrected, the text reads: ". . . had been
 arrested before advising Trinque . . . ."
