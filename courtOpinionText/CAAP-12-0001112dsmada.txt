Upon consideration of the Stipulation for Dismissal of 
Appeal With Prejudice, filed December 17, 2013, and the record,

'i Rules of Appellate 
Procedure Rule (HRAP) 42(b), and agree to bear their own costs and attorneys’ fees on appeal; (3) the appeal has not been docketed; and (4) dismissal is warranted under HRAP Rule 42(a). NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER

dismiss the appeal is approved and the appeal is dismissed with
 
prejudice. The parties shall bear their own appellate costs and
 attorneys’ fees.
