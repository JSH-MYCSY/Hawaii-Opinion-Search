SUMMARY DISPOSITION ORDER
 
(By: 
Foley, Presiding J., Leonard and Reifurth, JJ.)
 
Petitioner-Appellant James K. Trueman appeals from the
 
December 26, 2012 "Order Denying Without Hearing Petition To
 
Vacate, Set Aside Or Correct Judgment Or To Release Petition From
 Custody" of the Circuit Court of the First Circuit, the Honorable
 Richard K. Perkins presiding.
 
Upon careful review of the record and the briefs
 
submitted by the parties and having given due consideration to
 the arguments advanced and the issues raised by the parties, as
 well as the relevant statutory and case law, we conclude
 Trueman's appeal is without merit.
 
Therefore,
 NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the December 26, 2012 "Order
 
Denying Without Hearing Petition To Vacate, Set Aside Or Correct
 
Judgment Or To Release Petition From Custody" entered in the
 Circuit Court of the First Circuit is affirmed.
