The Memorandum Opinion of the court, filed on 
November 30, 2015, is hereby amended as follows:

reads: ". . . occurred four or five times . . . ."

should be corrected to read "Shimabukuro's". 
3. On page 13, in the last line of text,
 
"Shimbukuro's mother" should be corrected to read "Shimabukuro's
 mother".

 she described as "[v]ery strong.""
