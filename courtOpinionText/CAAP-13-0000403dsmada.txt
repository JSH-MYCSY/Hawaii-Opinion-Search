ORDER APPROVING THE AUGUST 20, 2013
AMENDED STIPULATION FOR DISMISSAL OF APPEAL WITH PREJUDICE 
(By: Nakamura, C.J., Leonard and Reifurth, JJ.) 
Upon consideration of the Amended Stipulation for 
Dismissal of Appeal With Prejudice, filed August 20, 2013, by 
Plaintiff-Appellant Melissa Lou Fletcher, the papers in support, and the record, it appears that (1) the amended stipulation is dated and signed by counsel for all parties; (2) the parties seek to dismiss the appeal with prejudice, pursuant to Hawai
'i Rules 
of Appellate Procedure (HRAP) Rule 42(b), but dismissal is warranted by HRAP Rule 42(a) because the appeal has not been docketed; and (3) the parties agree to bear their own costs and attorneys’ fees on appeal. Therefore, NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the amended stipulation to
 
dismiss the appeal with prejudice is approved and the appeal is
 
dismissed with prejudice. The parties shall bear their own
 appellate costs and attorneys’ fees.
