(By: Nakamura, Chief Judge, for the court )
The Opinion of the court, filed on May 13, 2016, is
 
hereby corrected as follows:
 
1. On page 10, in the last line of the page, the word
 
"possession" should be replaced with "possessing" so that as
 
corrected, the text reads: ". . . felons from possessing
 firearms, . . . ."
 
2. On page 11, in the sixth line of text from the
 
bottom of the page (the fourth line of the block quoted material
 near the bottom of page 11), the word "of" that appears between
 "firearm" and "ammunition" should be replaced with "or" so that
 as corrected, the text reads: ". . . any firearm or ammunition 
. . . ."
