On March 18, 2016, this court issued a Summary
 
Disposition Order in this appeal. On March 28, 2016 Defendant-
Appellant Ronald W. Benner (Benner) filed a "Motion for
 Publication of the Court's March 18, 2016 Summary Disposition
 Order" (Motion for Publication), seeking to have this court's
 Summary Disposition Order issued as a Published Opinion. On
 April 4, 2016, Plaintiff-Appellee JPMorgan Chase Bank, National
 Association (JPMorgan Chase) filed its opposition to the Motion
 for Publication. Upon consideration of Benner's Motion for
 Publication and JPMorgan Chase's opposition, 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that Benner's Motion for
 NOT FOR PUBLICATION  IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
 
Publication is granted.
 
IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that the March 18, 2016 Summary
 
Disposition Order of this court is rescinded and an opinion for
 
publication will follow. 
DATED: Honolulu, Hawai 'i, April 6, 2016.
