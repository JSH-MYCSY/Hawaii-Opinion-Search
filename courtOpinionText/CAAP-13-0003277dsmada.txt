Dismissal of Appeal and Order" (stipulation), filed by Defendant-

Appellee Bank of America, NA stipulate to dismiss Appeal No.
 
CAAP-13-0003277; (2) the attorneys for the parties have signed

 
the stipulation; (3) the appeal has not been docketed; and 
NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER

an appeal has not been docketed, the appeal shall be dismissed 
upon the filing of a stipulation for dismissal signed by all the parties[.]" 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that Appeal No. CAAP­
13-0003277 is dismissed.
