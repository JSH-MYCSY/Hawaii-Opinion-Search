APPEAL FROM THE FAMILY COURT OF THE FIRST CIRCUIT

ORDER DISMISSING APPEAL PURSUANT TO HRAP RULE 30
 
(By: Nakamura, C.J., Fujise and Reifurth, JJ.)

 
Upon review of the record, it appears that: 
(1) On February 11, 2014, Defendant-Appellant
 
Francis M. Nakamura, Jr. (Appellant) filed a notice of appeal;
 
(2) The record on appeal was filed on April 1, 2014,
 
and the appellate clerk informed Appellant that the opening brief
 was due by May 12, 2014; 
(3) Appellant obtained extensions of time to file the
 
opening brief, making the opening brief due on July 11, 2014. 
(4) On August 14, 2014, the appellate clerk informed 
Appellant that the time for filing the opening brief had expired and, pursuant to Hawai
'i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 30, 
the matter would be called to the court's attention on August 25, 2014, for such action as the court deems proper, which could include dismissal; and NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
 
(5) Thereafter, Appellant did not file the opening
 
brief or respond to the notice of default. 

Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the appeal is
dismissed. 
 
DATED: Honolulu, Hawai 'i, September 10, 2014.
