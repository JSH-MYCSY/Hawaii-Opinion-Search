ORDER DENYING MOTION FOR RECONSIDERATION
 
(By: Leonard, Presiding Judge, and Reifurth and Ginoza, JJ.)

 
On July 6, 2016, Plaintiff-Appellee Donnalyn M. Mosier
 
filed a motion for reconsideration. Mosier's motion for
 
reconsideration requests reconsideration of this court's Summary
 Disposition Order, which was filed on June 24, 2016.
 
Hawai
'i Rules of Appellate Procedure ("HRAP") Rule 40 
provides in relevant part: 
(a) Time. A motion for reconsideration may be filed by a
party only within 10 days after the filing of the opinion,
dispositional order, or ruling unless by special leave
additional time is granted during such period by a judge or
justice of the appellate court involved.
 
Mosier did not file her motion for reconsideration
 
within ten days after the filing of the Summary Disposition Order
 
as required by HRAP Rule 40.
 
Accordingly, pursuant to HRAP Rule 40(a), IT IS HEREBY
 
ORDERED that Mosier's motion for reconsideration is denied as
 untimely.
