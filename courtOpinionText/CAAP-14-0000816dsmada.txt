Upon consideration of the Stipulation and Order for
 
Dismissal of Appeal, filed on August 22, 2014 by Plaintiff-

stipulate to dismiss Appeal No. CAAP-14-0000816; (2) the parties and their attorneys have signed the stipulation; (3) the stipulation provides that the "[e]ach party shall bear their own respective fees and costs arising from this appeal"; (4) no fees are due; and (5) Hawai
'i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 42(b) 
provides, "If the parties to a docketed appeal or other proceeding sign and file a stipulation for dismissal, specifying NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
 
the terms as to payment of costs, and pay whatever fees are due,
 
the case shall be dismissed upon approval by the appellate
 court[.]"
 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that Appeal No. CAAP­
14-0000816 is dismissed. The parties shall bear their own
 appellate costs and fees.
