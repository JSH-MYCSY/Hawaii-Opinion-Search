STIPULATION TO DISMISS THE NOTICE OF APPEAL 

(By: Nakamura, C.J., Fujise and Reifurth, JJ.)
 
Upon consideration of the "Stipulation to Dismiss the
 
Notice of Appeal Filed on June 27, 2014" (Stipulation), which
 
this court construes as a stipulation to dismiss the appeal,
 filed by Plaintiff-Appellant Catherine A. Wilson (Appellant), on
 
October 31, 2014, and the record, it appears that (1) Appellant
 
and Defendant-Appellee David M. Wyle stipulate to dismiss the
 appeal; (2) the parties and their attorneys have signed the
 stipulation; (3) the record on appeal has not been filed; and
 (4) Hawai
'i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 42(a) provides, "If 
an appeal has not been docketed, the appeal shall be dismissed upon the filing of a stipulation for dismissal signed by all the parties." NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Stipulation is
 
approved and Appeal No. CAAP-14-0000910 is dismissed.
