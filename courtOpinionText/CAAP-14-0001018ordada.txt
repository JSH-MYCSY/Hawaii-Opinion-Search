On December 28, 2015, this court issued a Memorandum
 
Opinion in this appeal. On December 28, 2015, Defendants-
Appellants Daniel Smith and Tammy Smith (Smiths) filed
 
"Appellants' Motion for Publication of the Court's December 28,
 
2015 Memorandum Opinion" (Motion for Publication), seeking to
 
have this court's Memorandum Opinion issued as a Published
 
Opinion.
 
Upon consideration of the Smiths' Motion for
 
Publication and having no opposition being filed,
 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Smiths' Motion for
 
Publication is granted.
 
IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that the December 28, 2015 
NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
 
Memorandum Opinion of this court is rescinded and an opinion for 
publication will follow.
