Upon review of the record, it appears that: 
(1) On August 25, 2014, Petitioner-Appellant Douglas
 
Bitterman (Appellant), pro se, filed a notice of appeal;
 
(2) On September 30, 2014, the record on appeal was
 
filed, making the statement of jurisdiction due on October 10,
 2014, and the opening brief due on November 10, 2014;
 
(3) Thereafter, Appellant failed to file a statement of
 
jurisdiction or opening brief, and on November 19, 2014, the
 appellate clerk informed Appellant that the aforementioned
 deadlines had expired and the matter would be called to the

court's attention on December 1, 2014 for action that the court
 
deemed proper, which may include dismissal of the appeal. The
 appellate clerk notified Appellant that he could move for relief
 from the defaults. 
(4) Thereafter, Appellant did not file the statement of
 
jurisdiction or opening brief or respond to the notice of
 default. 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the appeal is 
dismissed.
