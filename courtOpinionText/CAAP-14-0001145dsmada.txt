Upon consideration of the Stipulation of Voluntary 
Dismissal of Appeal, submitted on June 22, 2015, by Plaintiff-
Appellee Federal Home Loan Mortgage Corporation, the papers in support, and the record, it appears that: (1) the stipulation is dated and signed by counsel for all parties appearing in the appeal; (2) the parties seek to dismiss the appeal, pursuant to Hawai
'i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 42(b); (3) the appeal 
was docketed December 2, 2014; and (4) the parties agree to bear their own costs and attorneys' fees.  NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the stipulation to
 
dismiss the appeal is approved and the appeal is dismissed. The
 
parties shall bear their own costs and attorneys' fees.
 
IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that all pending motions are
 
dismissed as moot.
