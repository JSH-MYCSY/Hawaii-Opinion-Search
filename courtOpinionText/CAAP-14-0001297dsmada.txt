ORDER DISMISSING APPEAL PURSUANT TO HRAP RULE 30
 
(By: Foley, Presiding Judge, Leonard and Ginoza, JJ.)

 
Upon review of the record, it appears that: 
(1) On November 13, 2014, Defendant-Appellant Patrick
 
Timothy Brent (Appellant), pro se, filed a notice of appeal;
 
(2) The record on appeal was filed December 24, 2014,
 
and the appellate clerk informed Appellant that the statement of
 jurisdiction was due by January 5, 2015, and the opening brief
 was due by February 2, 2015; 
(3) Appellant did not file either document; 
 NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER 
(4) On February 11, 2015, the appellate clerk informed 
Appellant that the time for filing the statement of jurisdiction 
and opening brief had expired and, pursuant to Hawai 'i Rules of 
Appellate Procedure Rule 30, the matter would be called to the court’s attention on February 23, 2015, for such action as the court deems proper, which could include dismissal; and 
(5) Thereafter, Appellant did not file the statement of
 
jurisdiction and opening brief, or respond to the notice of
 default. 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the appeal is 
dismissed.
