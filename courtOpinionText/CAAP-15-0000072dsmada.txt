Appeal, filed June 5, 2015, by Defendant-Appellant Garet Morrette

governs dismissal of docketed appeals; (3) the appeal was
 docketed March 17, 2015; and (4) the stipulation includes
 Appellant's declaration, consistent with HRAP Rule 42(c).

dismiss the appeal is approved and the appeal is dismissed.
