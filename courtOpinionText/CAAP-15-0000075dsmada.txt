Upon consideration of the Stipulation to Dismiss
 
Appeal, filed May 4, 2015, by Plaintiff-Appellant Cammi Ma, the
 
papers in support, and the record, it appears that (1) the
 stipulation is dated and signed by counsel for all parties; 
(2) the parties seek to dismiss the appeal with prejudice, pursuant to Hawai
'i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 42(b); 
(3) the appeal was docketed May 4, 2015; and (4) the parties
 agree to bear their own costs and attorneys' fees.

 fees.
