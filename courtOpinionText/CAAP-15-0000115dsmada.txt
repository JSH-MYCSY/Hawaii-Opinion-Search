Upon consideration of the Motion to Dismiss Appeal With 
Prejudice (Motion), filed June 3, 2015 by Defendant-Appellant 
Cathleen Long (Appellant), the papers in support, and the record, it appears that (1) the appeal was docketed May 4, 2015; (2) Hawai
'i Rules of Appellate Procedure (HRAP) Rule 42(b) authorizes 
the court to dismiss a docketed appeal upon a motion; (3) Appellant seeks to dismiss the appeal as moot; and (4) the motion includes Appellant's declaration showing she understands the consequences of voluntary dismissal, consistent with HRAP Rule 42(c).  NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Motion is
 
granted and the appeal is dismissed with prejudice.
