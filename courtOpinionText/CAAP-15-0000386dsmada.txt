(By: Nakamura, C.J., Foley and Fujise, JJ.) 
Upon consideration of Defendant-Appellant Selina 
Dalao's (Appellant) July 1, 2015 Motion to Dismiss Appeal, 
Appellant's July 7, 2015 Motion for Second Extension of Time to File Statement of Jurisdiction and Opening Brief, the papers in support, and the record, it appears that (1) the appeal was docketed May 29, 2015; (2) Hawai
'i Rules of Appellate Procedure 
(HRAP) Rule 42(b) authorizes the court to dismiss a docketed appeal upon a motion; (3) Appellant seeks to dismiss the appeal as moot; and (4) the motion includes Appellant's declaration showing she understands the consequences of voluntary dismissal,
 consistent with HRAP Rule 42(c).
 NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Motion to
 
Dismiss Appeal is granted and the appeal is dismissed.
 
IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that all other pending motions
 
are dismissed as moot.
