Upon consideration of the Stipulation for Dismissal of 
Appeal (Stipulation), filed November 3, 2015, by Defendant-
Appellant Kainoa Guadiz Ferreira (Appellant), the papers in support, and the record, it appears that (1) the appeal was docketed May 22, 2015; (2) Hawai
'i Rules of Appellate Procedure 
(HRAP) Rule 42(b) and (c) authorize the court to dismiss a docketed criminal appeal upon a stipulation; (3) the parties seek to dismiss this appeal by stipulation; (4) the Stipulation includes Appellant's declaration showing he understands the consequences of voluntary dismissal, consistent with HRAP Rule 42(c). approved and the appeal is dismissed.
