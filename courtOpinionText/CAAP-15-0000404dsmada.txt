(By: Nakamura, C.J., Fujise and Leonard, JJ.)
 
Upon consideration of the "Stipulation for Dismissal as
 
to the Notice of Appeal Filed May 15, 2015" (Stipulation for
 Dismissal) filed on April 6, 2016, and the record, it appears
 that: (1) Defendant-Appellant Roger Louis Kay and Plaintiff-
Appellee Central Mortgage Company stipulate to dismiss Appeal No.
 CAAP-15-0000404; (2) the parties' attorneys have signed the
 Stipulation for Dismissal; (3) the Stipulation for Dismissal
 provides that the parties are to pay their own costs and
 NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
 
attorney's fees; (4) the appeal has been docketed; (5) no payment 
is due; and (6) Hawai 'i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 42(b) 
provides, "If the parties to a docketed appeal or other proceeding sign and file a stipulation for dismissal, specifying the terms as to payment of costs, and pay whatever fees are due, the case shall be dismissed upon approval of the appellate court[.]" 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Stipulation
 
for Dismissal is approved, and Appeal No. CAAP-15-0000404 is
 dismissed.
