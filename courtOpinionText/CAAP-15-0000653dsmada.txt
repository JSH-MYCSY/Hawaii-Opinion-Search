(By: Foley, Presiding Judge, Leonard and Ginoza, JJ.)
 
Upon consideration of the Stipulation for Dismissal of
 
Appeal, filed June 14, 2016, by Defendants-Appellants Scott
 
Carlton Fleck and Kiendra Rae Fleck, the papers in support, and
 the record, it appears that (1) the parties stipulate to dismiss
 the appeal with prejudice, with each party to bear their own
 attorneys' fees and costs; (2) the stipulation is dated and
 signed by counsel for all parties appearing in the appeal; 
(3) the appeal has not been docketed; and (4) Hawai
'i Rules of 
Appellate Procedure Rule 42(a) authorizes dismissal of an appeal that has not been docketed. NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the stipulation is
 
approved and the appeal is dismissed with prejudice. Each party
 
shall bear their own attorneys' fees and costs.
