Hawaii's (Appellant) January 8, 2016 Motion to Withdraw Notice of 
Appeal (Motion), which the court construes as a motion to dismiss the appeal, pursuant to Hawai

(HRAP) Rule 42(b), Defendant-Appellee Ioane J. Teves' January 8, 2016 Statement of No Objection to the Motion, the papers in support, and the record, it appears that (1) the appeal was docketed on November 30, 2015; (2) Appellant seeks to dismiss the appeal because it decided not to pursue the appeal; and (3) HRAP Rule 42(b) authorizes the court to dismiss a docketed appeal upon a motion.  NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Motion is
 
granted and the appeal is dismissed.
