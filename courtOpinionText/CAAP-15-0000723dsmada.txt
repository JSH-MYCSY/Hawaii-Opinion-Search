Upon consideration of the Stipulation to Dismiss
 
Appeal, filed March 1, 2016, by Respondent-Appellant Tristan
 
Chitty, the papers in support, and the record, it appears that

'i Rules of 
Appellate Procedure Rule 42(b); (3) the stipulation is dated and signed by counsel for all parties appearing in the appeal; and (4) the parties agree to bear their own costs and attorneys'
 fees.

dismiss the appeal is approved and the appeal is dismissed. The
 
parties shall bear their own costs and attorneys' fees.
