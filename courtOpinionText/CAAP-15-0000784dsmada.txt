Upon consideration of the "Stipulation for Dismissal of
 
Appeal" filed on July 6, 2017, by Defendants-Appellants Gregory
 
Allen Bundy and Nicole Bundy, the papers in support, and the
 record, it appears that (1) the appeal was docketed on April 11,

 fees incurred with respect to this appeal.
