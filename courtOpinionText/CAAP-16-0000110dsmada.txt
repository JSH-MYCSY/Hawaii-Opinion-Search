Appeal, filed May 31, 2016, by Defendant-Appellant Michael Henry
 
Lipman, the papers in support, and the record, it appears that

Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the stipulation to 
dismiss the appeal is approved and the appeal is dismissed.
