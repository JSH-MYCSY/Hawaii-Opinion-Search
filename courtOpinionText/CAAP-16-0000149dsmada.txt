Upon consideration of Petitioner-Appellant Makimi

 
Magsanay's (Appellant) May 16, 2016 Motion to Dismiss Appeal
 
(Motion), Grantor-Appellee Yoshiko Shimabukuro's (Appellee
 Shimabukuro) May 18, 2016 Response to Motion to Dismiss Appeal,
 Trustee-Appellee Shin Domen's (Appellee Domen) May 19, 2016
 Response to Motion to Dismiss Appeal, the papers in support, and
 the record, it appears that (1) Appellant seeks to dismiss the
 appeal, with each party to bear their own appellate costs and
 attorneys' fees; (2) in response, Appellee Shimabukuro and
 Appellee Domen separately ask the court to dismiss the appeal
 NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER 
with prejudice and assess costs against Appellant but neither 
appellee provides details concerning costs incurred on appeal, or cites authority for the requested relief; (3) Hawai
'i Rules of 
Appellate Procedure Rule 42(b) authorizes the court, upon a motion, to dismiss a docketed appeal; and (4) the circuit court clerk docketed the appeal on May 2, 2016. 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Motion is
 
granted in part and denied in part as follows:
 
(1) The request to dismiss the appeal is granted and
 
the appeal is dismissed. Each party shall bear their own
 attorneys' fees on appeal.
 
(2) The request that each party shall bear their own
 
costs on appeal is denied without prejudice.
 
IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that Appellee Shimabukuro's and
 
Appellee Domen's separate requests to assess costs against
 Appellant are denied without prejudice to filing in this court a
 motion or separate motions detailing the costs incurred on appeal
 and authority for granting the requested relief.
