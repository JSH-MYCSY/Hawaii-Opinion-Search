ORDER APPROVING STIPULATION TO DISMISS APPEAL
 
(By: 
Fujise, Presiding Judge, Leonard and Ginoza, JJ.)
 
Upon consideration of the "Stipulation to Dismiss 
Appeal," filed on February 7, 2017, by Defendants-Appellees Dr. 
Anthony J. Mauro, M.D., and Jeffrey S. Portnoy, the papers in support, and the record, it appears that (1) the appeal was docketed on May 5, 2016; (2) the parties stipulate to dismiss the appeal with prejudice, pursuant to Hawai
'i Rules of Appellate 
Procedure (HRAP) Rule 42(b); (3) the stipulation is dated and signed by all parties or their counsel appearing in the appeal; and (4) the parties agree to bear their own costs and attorneys' fees incurred with respect to this appeal.  Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the stipulation to
 
dismiss the appeal is approved and the appeal is dismissed with
 
prejudice. The parties shall bear their own costs and attorneys'
 fees incurred with respect to this appeal.

'i, April 7, 2017.
