(By: Nakamura, C.J., Fujise and Reifurth, JJ.)

 
Upon review of the record, it appears that: 
(1) On April 8, 2016, Plaintiff-Appellant Chason Take
 
Ishii (Appellant) filed a notice of appeal;
 
(2) On May 7, 2016, the circuit court clerk filed the
 
record on appeal;
 
(3) On May 9, 2016, the appellate clerk notified the
 
parties that the statement of jurisdiction and opening brief were
 due on or before May 17, 2016, and June 16, 2016, respectively;
 
(4) Appellant did not file either document, or request
 
an extension of time;
 NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
 
(5) On July 12, 2016, the appellate clerk notified 
Appellant that the time for filing the statement of jurisdiction 
and opening brief had expired and, pursuant to Hawai 'i Rules of 
Appellate Procedure Rules 12.1(e) and 30, the matter would be called to the court’s attention on July 22, 2016, for appropriate action, which could include dismissal of the appeal; and 
(6) Thereafter, Appellant did not file the statement of
 
jurisdiction or opening brief, or otherwise respond to the
 default notice.
 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the appeal is 
dismissed.
