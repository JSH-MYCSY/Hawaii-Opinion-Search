Upon consideration of the document filed June 19, 2017, 
by Petitioner-Appellant Joseph P. Purtell, Jr. (Appellant), pro se, which the court construes as a motion to dismiss the appeal, pursuant to Hawai

(Motion), the papers in support, and the record, it appears that: 
(1) 
This appeal was docketed on September 10, 2016;
 
(2) Appellant seeks to dismiss the appeal; 
(3) On June 20, 2017, the court granted Respondent-
Appellee State of Hawaii’s (Appellee) June 13, 2017 Motion to
 Seal Records on Appeal. Among other things, the court ordered
 the appellate clerk to seal parts 1 and 2 of the record on
