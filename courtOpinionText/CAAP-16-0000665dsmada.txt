Upon consideration of the “Stipulation for Dismissal of
Appeal,” filed January 12, 2017, by Plaintiff/Counterclaim
Defendant-Appellee Deutsche Bank National Trust Company, the
papers in support, and the record, it appears that (1) the appeal

for all parties appearing in the appeal.2Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the stipulation to
dismiss the appeal is approved, and the appeal is dismissed.  The
parties shall bear their own costs and attorneys' fees on appeal.
