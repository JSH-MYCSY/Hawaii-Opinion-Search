Upon consideration of "Defendant-Appellant James B.
Nutter & Company's Unopposed Motion to Dismiss with Prejudice
Notice of Appeal, Filed on April 4, 2017" filed on January 8,
2019, the papers in support and the record, it appears that:
(1) an appeal was filed by James B. Nutter & Company on
April 4, 2017;

and Clay Chapman Iwamura Pulice & Nervell, Attorneys at Law, aNOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAII REPORTS OR THE PACIFIC REPORTER

appeal with prejudice, subject to each party bearing their own
costs and attorneys' fees with respect to the appeal;
(4) the motion is not opposed; and
(5) the dismissal of the appeal will not impact the
cross-appeal.
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion to
dismiss the appeal is granted and the appeal is dismissed with
prejudice.  The parties shall bear their own costs and attorneys'
fees incurred with respect to the appeal.  The cross-appeal
remains active and pending disposition.
