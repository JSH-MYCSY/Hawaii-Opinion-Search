National  Association's  January  6,  2024  Stipulation  for  Dismissal

parties  stipulate  to  dismiss  the  appeal  and  bear  their  own 
attorneys'  fees  and  costs  on  appeal;  (3)  the  stipulation  is  dated 
and  signed  by  counsel  for  all  parties  appearing  in  the  appeal  who          NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER

Hawai i  Rules   of   Appellate  Procedure  Rule  42(b). 
Therefore,  IT  IS  HEREBY  ORDERED  that  the  Stipulation  is 
approved  and  the  appeal  is  dismissed.   The  parties  shall  bear 
their  own  attorneys'  fees  and  costs  on  appeal.  
IT  IS  FURTHER  ORDERED  that  all  pending  motions  are 
dismissed.
