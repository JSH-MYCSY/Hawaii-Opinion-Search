ORDER APPROVING STIPULATION TO DISMISS APPEAL
 
(By: Ginoza, C.J., and Reifurth and Chan, JJ.)
 
Upon consideration of the Stipulation and Dismissal of 
Appeal With Prejudice, filed August 31, 2018, by Appellant-
Appellant Kathleen Choi Kumbroch, the papers in support, and the record, it appears that (1) the appeal has been docketed; (2) pursuant to Hawai' i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 42(b), the 
parties stipulate to dismiss the appeal with prejudice and bear their own attorneys' fees and costs; and (3) the stipulation is dated and signed by counsel for all parties appearing in the appeal. 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the stipulation to
 
dismiss the appeal is approved, and the appeal is dismissed with
 prejudice. The parties shall bear their own attorneys' fees and
 costs.
 
DATED: Honolulu, Hawai' i, October 17, 2018.
