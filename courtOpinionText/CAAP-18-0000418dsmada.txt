ORDER GRANTING MOTION TO DISMISS APPEAL 
(By:  Ginoza, Chief Judge, Fujise and Leonard, JJ.) 
Upon consideration of the Motion to Dismiss Appeal, 
filed May 3, 2019, by Plaintiff-Appellant Mark G. Valencia 
(Valencia), it appears that (1) the appeal has been docketed; 
(2) Valencia seeks to dismiss the appeal; (3) dismissal is 
authorized by HRAP Rule 42(b); and (4) there is no opposition. 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion to 
dismiss the appeal is granted, and the appeal is dismissed.  The 
parties shall bear their own attorneys' fees and costs on appeal.
