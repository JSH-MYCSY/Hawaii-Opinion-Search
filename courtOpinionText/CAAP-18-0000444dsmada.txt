Upon consideration of Defendant-Appellant Mark N.
Koehler's (Koehler) August 21, 2018 Motion to Voluntarily
Withdraw Appeal, the papers in support, and the record, it
appears that (1) on August 2, 2018, the district court clerk
docketed the appeal; (2) Koehler seeks to withdraw the appeal;
(3) the instant motion includes Koehler's declaration showing he
understands the consequences of voluntary dismissal, consistent
with Hawai #i Rules of Appellate Procedure (HRAP) Rule 42(c); and
(4) dismissal is authorized by HRAP Rule 42(b) and (c).
//
//NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI #I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion to
withdraw the appeal is granted, and the appeal is dismissed.
