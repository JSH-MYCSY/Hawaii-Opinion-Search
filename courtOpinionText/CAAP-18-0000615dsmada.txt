ORDER GRANTING MOTION TO DISMISS APPEAL
(By:  Chan, Presiding Judge, Hiraoka and Wadsworth, JJ.)
Upon consideration of Defendant-Appellant Gabriel
Stan's September 14, 2020 Motion to Dismiss Appeal, the papers in
support, and the record, it appears that (1) the appeal has been
docketed; (2) Stan seeks to dismiss the appeal; (3) attached to
the motion is Stan's declaration showing he understands the
consequences of voluntary dismissal, consistent with Hawai# i
Rules of Appellate Procedure (HRAP) Rule 42(c); and (4) dismissal
is authorized by HRAP Rule 42(b) and (c).
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion is
granted, and the appeal is dismissed.
DATED:  Honolulu, Hawai# i, September 17, 2020.
