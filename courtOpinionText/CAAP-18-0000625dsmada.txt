ORDER APPROVING STIPULATION TO DISMISS APPEAL 
(By:  Reifurth, Presiding Judge, Chan and Hiraoka, JJ.) 
Upon consideration of the "Stipulation for Dismissal of 
Liliha Professional Building, LLC's Appeal From the Order 
Granting Intervenors Delphine Wong and Deborah Kobayashi, Co-
Trustees of the Willie & Delphine Wong Trust's Motion to Dismiss 
Complaint, Filed on April 20, 2018, Filed July 13, 2018," filed 
June 5, 2019, by Plaintiff-Appellant/Cross-Appellee Liliha 
Professional Building, LLC (Liliha Professional Building), the 
papers in support, and the record, it appears that: 
(1) Liliha Professional Building filed the notice of 
appeal on August 10, 2018, and Defendants-Appellees/Cross-
Appellants Nada, Ono, Ka#anhe , Solomon & Hayashi LLP, Leilani 
Ka#anehe, Aaron K. Nada, and David Ono (Nada Ono) filed the 
notice of cross-appeal on October 1, 2018; NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI #I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER 
(2) The appeal and cross-appeal have been docketed; 
(3) The parties stipulate to dismiss Liliha 
Professional Building's appeal with prejudice and bear their own 
attorneys' fees and costs concerning Liliha Professional 
Building's appeal.  The parties further stipulate that Nada Ono's 
cross-appeal remains pending and the parties reserve their rights 
concerning attorneys' fees and costs in the cross-appeal; 
(4) The stipulation is dated and signed by counsel for 
all parties appearing in the appeal and cross-appeal; and 
(5) Dismissal of Liliha Professional Building's appeal 
is authorized by Hawai# i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 42(b). 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the stipulation to 
dismiss Liliha Professional Building's appeal is approved and the 
appeal is dismissed with prejudice.  The parties shall bear their 
own attorneys' fees and costs in Liliha Professional Building's 
appeal.  Nada Ono's cross-appeal remains pending and the parties 
may move for attorneys' fees and costs in the cross-appeal. 
DATED:  Honolulu, Hawai# i, June 12, 2019.
