(2)
 On September 6, 2022, Appellant filed a timely 
motion for reconsideration of the Opinion, pursuant to Hawai# i 
Rules of Appellate Procedure (HRAP) Rule 40. 
(3)
 The motion for reconsideration presents no point of 
law or fact that this court overlooked or misapprehended. See 
HRAP Rule 40(b). NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAII REPORTS OR THE PACIFIC REPORTER
