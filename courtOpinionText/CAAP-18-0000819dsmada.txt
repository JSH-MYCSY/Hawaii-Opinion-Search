ORDER GRANTING MOTION TO DISMISS APPEAL WITH PREJUDICE
(By:  Ginoza, Chief Judge, Fujise and Leonard, JJ.)
Upon consideration of the Motion to Dismiss Appeal With
Prejudice, filed January 30, 2019, by Defendant-Appellant Ashley
Wellman (Wellman), it appears that (1) the appeal has been
docketed; (2) Wellman seeks to dismiss the appeal with prejudice,
because it is moot; (3) attached to the motion is Wellman's
declaration showing she understands the consequences of voluntary
dismissal, consistent with Hawai# i Rules of Appellate Procedure
(HRAP) Rule 42(c); and (4) dismissal is authorized by HRAP Rule
42(b) and (c).
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion is
granted, and the appeal is dismissed with prejudice.
DATED:  Honolulu, Hawaii, February 15, 2019. #
