ORDER GRANTING MOTION TO DISMISS APPEAL 
(By:  Reifurth, Presiding Judge, Chan and Hiraoka, JJ.) 
Upon consideration of the Motion to Dismiss Appeal, 
filed June 4, 2019, by Defendant-Appellant Payton Rapozo 
(Rapozo), it appears that (1) the appeal has been docketed; 
(2) Rapozo seeks to dismiss the appeal; (3) attached to the 
motion is Rapozo's declaration showing he understands the 
consequences of voluntary dismissal, consistent with Hawai# i 
Rules of Appellate Procedure (HRAP) Rule 42(c); and (4) dismissal is authorized by HRAP Rule 42(b) and (c). 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion is 
granted, and the appeal is dismissed.
