Claimant-Appellee/Appellee [Adam L. Josiah ( Claimant)] Without
Prejudice from Appellant-Appellant Special Compensation Fund's
[(Appellant )] Appeal," filed August 20, 2019, by Appellant, it

appeal; (3)  the stipulation is dated and signed by counsel forall parties appearing in the appeal; and (4) dismissal is
authorized by Hawai# i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 42(b).
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the stipulation to
dismiss Claimant Adam L. Josiah as a party is approved.
