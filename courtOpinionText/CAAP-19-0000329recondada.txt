(By: Reifurth, Presiding Judge, Chan and Hiraoka, JJ.) 
Upon review of (1) the June 28, 2019 order dismissing 
appeal, (2) Claimant/Appellant/Appellant Kenneth M. Skahan's 
(Skahan) June 29, 2019 motion for reconsideration of the June 28, 
2019 order pursuant to Rule 40 of the Hawai# i Rules of Appellate 
Procedure (HRAP), and (3) the record, it appears that we did not overlook or misapprehend any points of law or fact when we 
dismissed this appeal for lack of appellate jurisdiction.  
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that Skahan's June 29, 
2019 HRAP Rule 40 motion for reconsideration of the June 28, 2019 
order dismissing appeal is denied.
