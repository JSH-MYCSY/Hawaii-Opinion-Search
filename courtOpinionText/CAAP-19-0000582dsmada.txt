jurisdiction over this appeal by self-represented Plaintiff-
Appellant Vickram Jaisingane ( Jaisingane ).  Jaisingane purports
to appeal from Jaisingane v. Ass'n of Apartment Owners of Ala Wai
Plaza Skyrise, Civil No. 78341, Circuit Court of the First
Circuit, State of Hawai# i.  The Hawai# i Supreme Court affirmed theNOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI #I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER

Jaisingane also purports to appeal from Jaisingane v.

State of Hawai#i.  Civil No. 85-3896 was dismissed by stipulation

Rules of Civil Procedure.  We lack appellate jurisdiction.  See
