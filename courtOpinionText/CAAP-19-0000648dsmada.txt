ORDER OF DISMISSAL 
(By: Leonard, Acting Chief Judge, Hiraoka and Nakasone, JJ.) 
On January 9, 2024, Plaintiff/Counterclaim Defendant-
Appellee MTGLQ Investors, L.P. filed a motion for order 
dismissing appeal in CAAP-19-0000648 (Motion to Dismiss). On 
January 11, 2024, this Court entered an Order to Show Cause 
ordering Defendants/Counterclaim Plaintiffs-Appellants Charles Alapati Leomiti Ioane, and Okesi Atuatasi Ioane aka Okesi A. Ioane, Individually and as Trustee of the Okesi A. Ioane 
Revocable Trust Dated February 11, 2011 (Appellants) to show 
cause why this appeal should not be dismissed as moot by no later 
than 14 days from the date of the order or January 25, 2024. No response by Appellants has been filed. 
Upon review of the Motion to Dismiss, the Order to Show 
Cause, and the records and files herein, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Motion to Dismiss is granted. The appeal filed in CAAP-19-0000648 is hereby dismissed.
