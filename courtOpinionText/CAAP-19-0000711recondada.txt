ORDER DENYING MOTION FOR RECONSIDERATION
(By:  Leonard, Presiding Judge, Chan and Hiraoka, JJ.)
Upon review of the motion for reconsideration filed by
Appellant AM on October 5, 2020, and the record on appeal, it
appears that AM does not "present new evidence and/or arguments
that could not have been presented during the earlier"
proceedings.  See Chen v. Mah, 146 Hawai#i 157, 172, 457 P.3d
796, 811 (2020).  Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion
for reconsideration is denied.
