Appeal, filed September 9, 2020, by Petitioner-Appellant William

appeal; and (4) dismissal is authorized by Hawai# i Rules of
Appellate Procedure (HRAP) Rule 42(b).1

Appeal.NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI #I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
