(By:  Ginoza, Chief Judge, Fujise and Leonard, JJ.)
Upon consideration of the "Stipulation Withdrawing
Appeal and Order," filed March 30, 2020, by Defendant-Appellant
Frederick Nitta, it appears that (1) the appeal has not been
docketed; (2) pursuant to Hawai)i Rules of Appellate Procedure
(HRAP) Rule 42(b), the parties stipulate to dismiss the appeal
and bear their own costs and attorneys' fees; (3) the stipulation
is dated and signed by counsel for all parties appearing in the
appeal; and (4) because the appeal has not been docketed,
dismissal is authorized by HRAP Rule 42(a).
