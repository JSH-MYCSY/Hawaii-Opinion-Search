(By:  Ginoza, Chief Judge, Leonard and Wadsworth, JJ.)
Upon consideration of the December 14, 2020 Motion
Requesting Review of Certified Record on Appeal and Certified
Transcripts on record, by self-represented Plaintiff-Appellant
CH, which the court construes as a motion for reconsideration of
the December 4, 2020 Order Granting Motion to Dismiss Appeal, the
papers in support, and the record, it appears the court did not
overlook or misapprehend any point of law or fact when it entered
the December 4, 2020 order.  Hawai#i Rules of Appellate Procedure
Rule 40(b).
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion for
reconsideration is denied.
