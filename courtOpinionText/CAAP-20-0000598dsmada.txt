ORDER APPROVING STIPULATION TO DISMISS APPEAL
(By:  Ginoza, Chief Judge, Leonard and Chan, JJ.)
Upon consideration of the Stipulation to Dismiss
Appeal, filed October 22, 2020, by Father-Appellant BV, the
papers in support, and the record, it appears that (1) the appeal
has not been docketed; (2) the parties stipulate to dismiss the
appeal and that BV "shall be responsible for the payment of all
related costs and fees"; (3) the Stipulation to Dismiss Appeal is
dated and signed by all self-represented parties and counsel for
all other parties appearing in the appeal; and (4) dismissal is
authorized by Hawai#i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 42(a).
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Stipulation to
Dismiss Appeal is approved and the appeal is dismissed.  BV shall
be responsible for the payment of all related costs and fees.
DATED:  Honolulu, Hawai#i, October 26, 2020.
