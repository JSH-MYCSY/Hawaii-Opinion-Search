Appellant Roberta Wilborn's ( Wilborn) July 8, 2021 "Motion to

the motion for reconsideration within ten days after the court
entered the Order, as required by Hawai#i Rules of Appellate
Procedure Rule (HRAP ) Rule 40(a); and (3) regardless, Wilborn has
failed to present any point of law or fact that the courtNOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI #I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER

Rule 40(b).
