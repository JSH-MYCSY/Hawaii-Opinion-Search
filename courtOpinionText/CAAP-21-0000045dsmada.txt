(By: Ginoza, Chief Judge, and Leonard and Wadsworth, JJ.)
Upon consideration of the "Notice of Larry Card's
Withdrawal of Appeal Due to Severe Health Problems" filed on
January 19, 2022, by self-represented Plaintiff-Appellant Larry
Card (Appellant), the record, and there being no opposition, we
construe Appellant's notice to be a motion to dismiss the appeal
pursuant to Hawai# i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 42(b). 
Therefore,
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion is granted and the
appeal is dismissed.
