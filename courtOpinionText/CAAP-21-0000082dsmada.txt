ORDER GRANTING MOTION TO DISMISS APPEAL
(By:  Ginoza, Chief Judge, Nakasone and Fujise, JJ.)
Upon review of Defendant-Appellant Guy Brooks
Jaramillo's September 2, 2021 Motion to Dismiss Appeal, the
papers in support, and the record, it appears that (1) the appeal
has been docketed; (2) Jaramillo seeks to dismiss the appeal;
(3) attached to the motion is Jaramillo's declaration showing he
understands the consequences of voluntary dismissal, consistent
with Hawai#i Rules of Appellate Procedure (HRAP) Rule 42(c); and
(4) dismissal is authorized by HRAP Rule 42(b) and (c).
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion is
granted, and the appeal is dismissed.
DATED:  Honolulu, Hawai# i, September 8, 2021.
