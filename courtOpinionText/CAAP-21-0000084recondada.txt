ORDER DENYING MOTION FOR RECONSIDERATION
(By:  Ginoza, Chief Judge, Hiraoka and Nakasone, JJ.)
Upon consideration of self-represented Defendant-
Appellant Jan Moxley's ( Moxley) May 25, 2021 motion for
reconsideration of the court's May 13, 2021 Order Granting Motion
To Dismiss Appeal, the papers in support, and the record, it
appears that the motion for reconsideration is untimely, and in
any event, the court did not overlook or misapprehend any point
of law or fact when it entered the May 13, 2021 order.  See
Hawai#i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rules 40(a) and (b).
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion for
reconsideration is denied.
