ORDER GRANTING MOTION TO DISMISS APPEAL
(By:  Leonard, Presiding Judge, Wadsworth and Nakasone, JJ.)
Upon review of Defendant-Appellant Gary R. Gadient's
(Gadient) November 8, 2021 Motion to Dismiss Appeal, the papers
in support, and the record, it appears that (1) the appeal has
been docketed; (2) Gadient seeks to dismiss the appeal;
(3) attached to the motion is Gadient's declaration showing he
understands the consequences of voluntary dismissal, consistent
with Hawai#i Rules of Appellate Procedure ( HRAP) Rule 42(c); and
(4) dismissal is authorized by HRAP Rule 42(b) and (c).
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion is
granted, and the appeal is dismissed.
DATED:  Honolulu, Hawai# i, December 6, 2021.
