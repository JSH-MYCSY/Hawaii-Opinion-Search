ORDER APPROVING STIPULATION TO DISMISS APPEAL 
(By:  Wadsworth, Presiding Judge, and Nakasone and McCullen, JJ.) 
Upon consideration of the Stipulation for Dismissal 
with Prejudice of Appeal, filed on June 22, 2022, and the records 
and files herein, it appears that:  (1) the appeal has been docketed; (2) the parties stipulate to dismiss the appeal with prejudice pursuant to 
Hawai# i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 
42(b) and bear their own attorneys' fees and costs; and (3) the stipulation is dated and signed by counsel for all parties in this appeal. 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Stipulation 
for Dismissal with Prejudice of Appeal is approved and the appeal is dismissed with prejudice.  The parties shall bear their own attorneys' fees and costs.
