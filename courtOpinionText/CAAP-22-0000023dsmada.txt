(By: Ginoza, Chief Judge, Leonard and Nakasone, JJ.) 
Upon consideration of the "Stipulation to Dismiss with 
Prejudice Defendant and Crossclaimant-Appellee Romspen Investment 
Corporation and Claims Related to Parcel D-1-B-2" (Stipulation) 
filed on August 31, 2023, by Defendant and Crossclaimant-Appellee 
Romspen Investment Corporation (Romspen Investment), the papers 
in support, and the record, it appears that: NOT FOR PUBLICATION  IN WEST'S HAWAI #I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER 
(1) the appeal has been docketed; 
(2) pursuant to Hawai# i Rules of Appellate Procedure 
Rule 42(b), the parties stipulate: 
(a) to dismiss with prejudice "all claims asserted 
in this appeal by and against [Romspen Investment] and related to that certain parcel of real property located at 68-4747 Queen Kaahumanu Highway in the County and State of Hawai# i, commonly known as Parcel D-1-B-2, and 
also identified by Tax Map Key No. (3)6-8-001-036" (
Dismissed Claims);" 
(b) to bear their own attorneys' fees and costs as 
to the Dismissed Claims; 
(c)
 that all appearing parties have signed the 
Stipulation to Dismiss; 
(d) that "all other claims among/between the 
parties in this action are not affected by this Stipulation[;]" and (3) the Stipulation is dated and signed by counsel for 
all parties appearing in the appeal. 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Stipulation is 
approved and the Dismissed Claims are dismissed with prejudice. The parties shall bear their own attorneys' fees and costs as to the Dismissed Claims. All other claims among or between the parties in this action are not affected by this Stipulation.
