ORDER 
(By:  Wadsworth, Presiding Judge, Nakasone and McCullen, JJ.) 
 
  Upon consideration of Claimant-Appellant-Appellant Augustina J. Dean's (
Dean) Motion for Reconsideration filed on 
July 3, 2023, pursuant to Hawai‘ i Rules of Appellate Procedure 
(
HRAP) Rule 40, and the record and files herein, it appears 
that:      (1) Dean does not "present new evidence and/or 
arguments that could not have been presented" earlier, Chen v. 
Mah, 146 Hawai‘ i 157, 172, 457 P.3d 796, 811 (2020); and 
  (2) Dean does not present any point of law or 
fact that we overlooked or misapprehended.  See HRAP 40(b). 
  Therefore, IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that the Motion for Reconsideration is denied. 
  DATED:  Honolulu, Hawai‘ i, July 11, 2023.
