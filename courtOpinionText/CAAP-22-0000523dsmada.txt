(By:  Hiraoka, Presiding Judge, Nakasone and McCullen, JJ.) 
  Upon consideration of the Stipulation to Dismiss 
Appeal ( Stipulation ), filed March 13, 2024, by Defendant-Counter 
Claimant-Cross Claimant-Appellant Kalani Honua, Inc., the papers 
in support, and the record, it appears that (1) the appeal was 
docketed; (2) the parties stipulated to dismiss the appeal, 
under Hawai ‘i Rules of Appellate Procedure ( HRAP) Rule 42(b), and 
have addressed attorneys' fees and costs; and (3) the 
stipulation is dated and signed by counsel for all parties appearing in the appeal. 
 
 Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the stipulation 
is approved and the appeal is dismissed.  The parties shall bear their own attorneys' fees and costs on appeal.
