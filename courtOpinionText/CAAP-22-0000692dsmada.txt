ORDER GRANTING MOTION TO DISMISS APPEAL
(By:  Ginoza, Chief Judge, Wadsworth and Chan, JJ.)
Upon consideration of the Motion to Dismiss Appeal,
filed May 5, 2023 (motion), by Defendant-Appellant Damien Paleka
(Paleka), the papers in support, the record, and noting no
opposition, it appears that (1) the appeal was docketed on
November 15, 2022; (2) Paleka seeks to dismiss the appeal; (3)
the motion is supported by Paleka's declaration showing Paleka
understands the consequences of voluntary dismissal, consistent
with Hawai#i Rules of Appellate Procedure (HRAP) Rule 42(c); and
(4) dismissal is authorized by HRAP Rule 42(b) and (c).
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion is
granted, and the appeal is dismissed.
DATED:  Honolulu, Hawai# i, May 30, 2023.
