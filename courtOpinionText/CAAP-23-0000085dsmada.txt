        ORDER APPROVING STIPULATION TO DISMISS APPEAL 
(By: Hiraoka, Presiding Judge, Wadsworth and Guidry, JJ.) 
Upon
 consideration of the November 20, 2023 "Joint 
Stipulation to Dismiss Consolidated Appeal; Order" (Stipulation ) 
filed by Respondent-Appellant M.C., the papers in support, and 
the record, it appears that: (1) the appeals in CAAP-23-0000085 
and CAAP-23-0000214 are consolidated under CAAP-23-0000085; (2) 
the appeal has been docketed; (3) the parties stipulate to 
dismiss the consolidated appeal and bear their own attorneys' 
fees and costs on appeal; (4) the stipulation is dated and signed 
by counsel for all parties appearing in the appeal; and (5) 
dismissal is authorized by Hawai i Rules  of Appellate Procedure 
Rule 42(b).
