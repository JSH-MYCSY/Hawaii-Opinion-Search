ORDER APPROVING STIPULATION FOR DISMISSAL OF APPEAL
(By:  Leonard, Presiding Judge, Nakasone and McCullen, JJ.)
Upon consideration of the Stipulation for Dismissal of
Appeal, filed July 17, 2023, by Defendant-Appellant Norman R.
Wassman, IV (Wassman), which the court construes as a motion to
dismiss appeal because it is not signed by all parties, the
papers in support, and the record, it appears that (1) the appeal
was docketed on May 16, 2023; (2) pursuant to Hawai#i Rules of
Appellate Procedure (HRAP) Rule 42(c), Wassman requests that the
appeal be dismissed; (3) the stipulation is dated and signed by
counsel for Wassman only, who represents that the counsel for the
Plaintiff-Appellee State of Hawai#i waived signature on the
stipulation and does not object to dismissal of the appeal; (4)
the stipulation includes Wassman's declaration showing he
understands the consequences of voluntary dismissal, consistentNOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI #I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
with HRAP Rule 42(c); and (5) dismissal is authorized by HRAP
Rule 42(b) and (c).
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the appeal is
dismissed.
