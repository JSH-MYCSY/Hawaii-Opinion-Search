Upon review of the record, it appears that: 
(1)
 On April 21, 2023, self-represented Plaintiff-
Appellant William Gilroy (Gilroy) filed a notice of appeal in 
this court, which created appeal CAAP-23-0000302; 
(2) Gilroy failed to file a statement of jurisdiction, 
opening brief, or a request an extension of time to file either; 
(3)
 On July 18, 2023, the appellate clerk entered a 
default notice informing Gilroy that the time for filing the 
jurisdictional statement and opening brief expired and the matter would be called to the court's attention on July 28, 2023, for appropriate action which could include dismissal of the appeal under 
Hawai#i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rules 12.1(e) and 30, 
and Gilroy could request relief from default by motion; 
(4)
 The appellate clerk mailed the default notice to 
Gilroy at his address on record; and NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI #I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER 
(5) Gilroy took no action to seek relief from default. 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the appeal is 
dismissed. 
IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that all pending motions are 
dismissed.
