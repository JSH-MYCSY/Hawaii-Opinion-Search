(By:  Leonard, Presiding Judge, Nakasone and McCullen, JJ.)
Upon consideration of the Stipulation to Dismiss
Appellee Hawai#i Law Enforcement Federal Credit Union With
Prejudice (Stipulation), filed July 14, 2023, by Defendant-
Appellee Hawai#i Law Enforcement Federal Credit Union, the papers
in support, and the record, it appears that: (1) the filing fees
have been paid, but the appeal has not been docketed; (2) the
parties stipulate to dismiss Hawai#i Law Enforcement Federal
Credit Union with prejudice and agree that each party is
responsible for its own fees and costs relative to the
Stipulation; (3) the Stipulation is dated and signed by counsel
for all parties; and (4) dismissal is authorized by Hawai# i Rules
of Appellate Procedure Rule 42(a).Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Stipulation is
approved and the appeal is dismissed with prejudice as to Hawai# i
Law Enforcement Federal Credit Union.  Each party is responsible
for its own fees and costs related to the dismissal of Hawai#i
Law Enforcement Federal Credit Union.
