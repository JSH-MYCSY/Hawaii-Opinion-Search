ORDER APPROVING STIPULATION FOR DISMISSAL OF APPEAL
(By:  Leonard, Presiding Judge, Nakasone and McCullen, JJ.)
Upon consideration of the Stipulation to Dismiss Appeal
(Stipulation), filed on July 18, 2023, by Plaintiff-Appellant VS,
the papers in support, and the record, it appears that:  (1) the
filing fees have been paid, but the appeal has not been docketed;
(2) the parties stipulate to dismiss the appeal with no award of
costs or fees to either party; (3) the Stipulation is dated and
signed by counsel for all parties; and (4) dismissal is
authorized by Hawai#i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 42(a).
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Stipulation is
approved, and the appeal is dismissed.  There shall be no award
of costs or fees to either party.
