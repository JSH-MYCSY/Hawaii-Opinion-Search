 January 5, 2024 Stipulation 
for Dismissal of Appeal (Stipulation ) filed by Defendant-
Appellant Kasey Nobriga-Pacheco, the papers in support, and the

the Stipulation is supported by a declaration that reflects a 
knowing and intelligent understanding of the consequences of the 
dismissal of the appeal and that the dismissal is made 
voluntarily; and (5) dismissal is authorized by Hawai i Rules of 
Appellate
 Procedure Rule 42(b) and (c).
