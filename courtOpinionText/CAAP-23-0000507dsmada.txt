Upon consideration
 of the Stipulation for Dismissal 
with Prejudice of Defendants Trina Ellen Kalaniminoaka Mei Junn 
Wegesend and Warren Robert Wegesend's Notice of Appeal, Filed 
August 29, 2023 (Stipulation), filed June 4, 2024, by Defendants-

prejudice and bear their own attorneys' fees and costs on appeal; 
and (3) the Stipulation is signed by counsel for all parties 
appearing in the appeal.          NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER

approved  and  the  appeal  is  dismissed  with  prejudice.   The  parties 
shall  bear  their  own  attorneys'  fees  and  costs  on  appeal.
