ORDER  GRANTING  MOTION  TO  DISMISS  APPEAL 
(By:   Leonard,  Acting  Chief  Judge,  Hiraoka  and  Guidry,  JJ.) 
Upon  consideration   of  Defendant-Appellant  Duran 
Sekona's  (Sekona)  April  17,  2024  Motion  for  Dismissal  of  the 
Instant  Appeal,  the  papers  in  support,  and  the  record,  it  appears 
that  (1)  the  appeal  has  been  docketed;  (2)  Sekona  seeks  to 
dismiss  the  appeal;  and  (3)  attached  to  the  motion  is  Sekona's 
declaration  showing  he  understands  the  consequences  of  voluntary 
dismissal,  consistent  with  Hawai i  Rules  of   Appellate  Procedure 
(HRAP)  Rule  42(c).   Therefore,  IT  IS  HEREBY  ORDERED  that  the 
motion  is  granted  and  the  appeal  is  dismissed,  under  HRAP  Rule 
42(b)-(c).
