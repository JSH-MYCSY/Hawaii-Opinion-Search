ORDER  APPROVING  STIPULATION  TO  DISMISS  APPEAL 
(By:   Leonard,  Acting  Chief  Judge,  Hiraoka   and  McCullen,   JJ.) 
Upon consideration
 of the Stipulation to Dismiss Appeal 
(Stipulation), filed September 25, 2024, by Plaintiffs-Appellants 
Thomas M. Bell and Elizabeth Bell, the papers in support, and the 
record, it appears that (1) the appeal has been docketed and the 
filing fees have been paid; (2) under Hawai i  Rules of  Appellate 
Procedure Rule 42(b), the parties stipulate to dismiss the appeal 
and bear their own attorneys' fees and costs on appeal; and (3) 
the Stipulation is signed by counsel for all parties. 
Therefore,  IT   IS  HEREBY  ORDERED  that  the  Stipulation  is 
approved  and  the  appeal  is  dismissed.   The  parties  shall  bear 
their  own  attorneys'  fees  and  costs.
