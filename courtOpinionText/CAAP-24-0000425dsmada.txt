(By:   Leonard,  Acting  Chief  Judge,  Hiraoka  and  Nakasone,  JJ.) 
Upon consideration
 of the Stipulation for Dismissal 
with Prejudice (Stipulation), filed November 4, 2024, by 
Defendants-Appellees Inova Health Solutions LLC, Inova Care LLC, 
Inova International LLC, and John Le Boeuf, the papers in 
support, and the record, it appears that (1) the appeal has been 
docketed and the filing fees have been paid; (2) under Hawai i 
Rules
 of Appellate Procedure Rule 42(b), the parties stipulate to 
dismiss the appeal with prejudice and bear their own attorneys' 
fees and costs; and (3) the Stipulation is signed by counsel for 
all appearing parties.          
        
          
       
      NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Stipulation is 
approved and the appeal is dismissed with prejudice. The parties 
shall bear their own attorneys' fees and costs.
