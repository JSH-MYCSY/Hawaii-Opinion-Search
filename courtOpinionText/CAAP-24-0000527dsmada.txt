(By:   Leonard,  Acting  Chief  Judge,  Hiraoka  and  McCullen,  JJ.) 
Upon
 consideration of the Stipulation for Dismissal of 
Appeal (Stipulation), filed August 23, 2024, by Respondent-
Appellant Administrative Director of the Courts, the papers in 
support, and the record, it appears that (1) under Hawai i Rules  
of Appellate Procedure (HRAP) Rule 42(a), the parties stipulate 
to dismiss the appeal; (2) the Stipulation is signed by counsel 
for all parties; (3) the district court clerk subsequently 
docketed the appeal; and (4) dismissal is authorized by HRAP 
Rule 42(b). 
Therefore,  IT  IS  HEREBY  ORDERED  that  the  Stipulation  is 
approved  and  the  appeal  is  dismissed.
