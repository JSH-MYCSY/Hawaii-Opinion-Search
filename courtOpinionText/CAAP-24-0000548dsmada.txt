ORDER  APPROVING   STIPULATION   TO  DISMISS   APPEAL 
(By:   Leonard,   Acting  Chief  Judge,  Nakasone   and  Guidry,   JJ.) 
Upon
 consideration of the Stipulation to Dismiss Appeal 
(Stipulation), filed November 20, 2024, by Respondent-Appellant 
Geoffrey Stephen Scott, the papers in support, and the record, it 
appears that (1) the appeal has been docketed and the filing fees 
have been paid; (2) under Hawai i Rules  of Appellate Procedure 
Rule 42(b), the parties stipulate to dismiss the appeal and bear 
their own attorneys' fees and costs; and (3) the Stipulation is 
signed by all self-represented parties and counsel for all other 
appearing parties. 
Therefore,  IT  IS  HEREBY  ORDERED  that  the  Stipulation  is 
approved  and  the  appeal  is  dismissed.   The  parties  shall  bear 
their  own  attorneys'  fees  and  costs.
