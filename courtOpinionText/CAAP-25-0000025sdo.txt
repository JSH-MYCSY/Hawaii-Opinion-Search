On this record, we conclude that the KPC did not 
conduct a contested case hearing on Appellant's Petition, and a contested case hearing was not required by statute, 
administrative rule, or constitutional due process. We 
therefore vacate the circuit court's Order, KPC Order, Costs 
Order, and Final Judgment, and remand with instructions for the 
circuit court to enter an order dismissing Appellants’ appeal 
for lack of jurisdiction.
