(By:   Leonard,  Acting  Chief  Judge,  Hiraoka  and  Wadsworth,  JJ.) 
Upon  consideration   of  the  Stipulation  for  Dismissal  of 
Appeal,  filed  May  27,  2025,  by  Defendant/Counterclaimant-
Appellant  Benjamin  Predpelski,  the  papers  in  support,  and  the 
record,  it  appears  that  (1)  the  appeal  has  been  docketed; 
(2)  under  Hawai i   Rules  of  Appellate  Procedure  Rule  42(b),  the 
parties  stipulate  to  dismiss  the  appeal  with  prejudice  and  bear 
their  own  fees  and  costs  on  appeal;  and  (3)  the  Stipulation  is 
signed  by  counsel  for  all  appearing  parties. 
Therefore,  IT   IS  HEREBY  ORDERED  that  the  Stipulation  is 
approved  and  the  appeal  is  dismissed  with  prejudice.   The  parties 
shall  bear  their  own  fees  and  costs  on  appeal.
