(2) The Notice states, pursuant to HRAP Rule 50(b),

(3) HRAP Rule 50(b) does not apply here because there

a default notice informing Robertson that the time for filing 
the statement of jurisdiction and opening brief had expired, the 
matter would be called to the court's attention on February 20, 
2026, for appropriate action, which could include dismissal of 
the appeal, under HRAP Rules 12.1(e) and 30, and Robertson could 
request relief from default by motion; and 
(7) Robertson has not requested relief from default or 
an extension of time to file the opening brief. 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Notice, 
construed as a motion to withdraw as counsel, is granted.
