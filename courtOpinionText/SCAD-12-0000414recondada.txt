On March 8, 2013, Respondent Wing C. Ng filed with this 
court a document entitled “Request for Reconsideration; Request 
for Clarification; Previous Reply to ODC.” Though Respondent Ng 
fails to cite to any authority for the filing, we deem it a 
motion for reconsideration, timely made pursuant to Rule 40 of 
the Hawai' i Rules of Appellate Procedure. 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion is denied. DATED: Honolulu, Hawai' i, March 18, 2013.
