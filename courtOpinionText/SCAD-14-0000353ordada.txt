Upon consideration of the July 12, 2021 declaration and
renewed application for reinstatement, filed by Respondent
Stephen C. Woodruff, the July 19, 2021 response filed by the
Office of Disciplinary Counsel, and the entire record in this
matter, we conclude that Respondent Woodruff’s July 12, 2021
submission is sufficient to fulfill the requirements of Rule
2.17(b)(2) of the Rules of the Supreme Court of the State of
Hawaiʻi (RSCH).  Therefore, 
 IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that Respondent Woodruff’s
application for reinstatement is granted. Electronically Filed
