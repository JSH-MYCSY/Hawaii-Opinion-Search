SCAD-15-0000333
IN THE SUPREME COURT OF THE STATE OF HAWAI#I
In re: JUNE C. IKEMOTO (Deceased)
(Bar No. 3689)
ORIGINAL PROCEEDING
ORDER
(By: Recktenwald, C.J., Nakayama, McKenna, Wilson, and Eddins, JJ.)
Upon consideration of the March 15, 2021 report and
motion for discharge, filed by Chief Disciplinary Counsel Bradley
Tamm, as Successor Trustee, pursuant to Rule 2.20 of the Rules of
the Supreme Court of the State of Hawai#i (RSCH), of the law
practice of deceased attorney June C. Ikemoto, and the record in
this matter, we conclude that nothing further remains to be done
in this trusteeship.  Therefore, 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion is granted.  Tamm
is hereby discharged as trustee in this matter.
IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that the clerk of the court shall
close this case.
DATED: Honolulu, Hawai# i, March 23, 2021.
