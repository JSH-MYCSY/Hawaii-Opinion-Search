filed by Chief Disciplinary Counsel of the Office of Disciplinary
Counsel Bradley Tamm, as Successor Trustee, pursuant to Rule 2.20
of the Rules of the Supreme Court of the State of Hawai&i (RSCH),
over the legal practice of deceased attorney Thomas P. Dunn, and
the record in this matter, we conclude that nothing further
remains to be done in this trusteeship.  Therefore,
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that Successor Trustee Tamm is
discharged.Electronically Filed
