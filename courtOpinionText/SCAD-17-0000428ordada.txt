ORDER OF REINSTATEMENT
(By: Recktenwald, C.J., Nakayama, McKenna, Pollack, and Wilson, JJ.)
Upon consideration of the January 17, 2018 petition and
affidavit submitted by Respondent Jamae K.K. Kawauchi and the
record, we conclude Respondent Kawauchi has complied with the
terms of her suspension.  Therefore,
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that, pursuant to RSCH Rule
2.17(b), Respondent Kawauchi is reinstated to the practice of law
in the jurisdiction of the State of Hawai #i, effective upon entry
of this order.
DATED: Honolulu, Hawaii, January 25, 2018. #
