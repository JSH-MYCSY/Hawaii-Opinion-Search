ORIGINAL PROCEEDING
ORDER DISMISSING ELECTION COMPLAINT
(By: Recktenwald, C.J., Nakayama, McKenna and Wilson, JJ.,
and Circuit Judge Eddins, assigned by reason by vacancy)
Upon consideration of plaintiff Khistina Caldwell
DeJean’s election complaint, filed on August 7, 2020, respondent
Scott Nago’s motion to dismiss the complaint, filed on August 14,
2020, the documents attached thereto and submitted in support
thereof, and the record, it appears that this court lacks
jurisdiction to consider the complaint.  See HRS § 602-5; see
also HRS § 11-172.  Accordingly, 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion to dismiss is
granted and the complaint is dismissed.
