ORIGINAL PROCEEDING 
ORDER DENYING MOTION FOR RECONSIDERATION 
(By: Recktenwald, C.J., Nakayama, McKenna, Wilson, and Eddins, JJ.) 
Upon consideration of the motion for reconsideration of 
the August 26, 2022 Findings of Fact, Conclusions of Law, and 
Judgment, which was filed by Plaintiff Daniel B. Decker IV on August 30, 2022, and the record, it does not appear this court has overlooked or misapprehended points of law or fact.  See Hawai# i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 40(b).  Accordingly, it 
is ordered that the motion for reconsideration is denied.
