ORDER DENYING MOTION FOR RECONSIDERATION 
(By: Recktenwald, C.J., Nakayama, McKenna, Wilson, and Eddins, JJ.) 
  
  Upon consideration of the motion for reconsideration 
of the September 6, 2022 Findings of Fact, Conclusions of Law, and Judgment, which was timely filed on September 14, 2022 by 
Plaintiff Jay Dee Penn, and the record in this matter, the court 
has not overlooked or misapprehended points of law or fact.  See 
Hawaiʻi Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 40(b).  Accordingly, it 
is ordered that the motion for reconsideration is denied.
