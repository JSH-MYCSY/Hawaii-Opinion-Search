ORIGINAL PROCEEDING
ORDER DISMISSING WITHOUT PREJUDICE “MOTION FOR NEW TRIAL”
(By: Recktenwald, C.J., Nakayama, Acoba, McKenna, and Pollack, JJ.)
Upon consideration of the document entitled “Motion for
New Trial,” which was filed by Mary Kaneakua on August 15, 2013,
and the record, it appears that this court does not have
jurisdiction to provide the relief requested in the motion. 
Therefore, 
 IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion is dismissed
without prejudice.
