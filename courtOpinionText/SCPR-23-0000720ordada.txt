IN RE CANDICE ANDREA GARCIA-RODRIGO, Petitioner.  
ORIGINAL PROCEEDING  
 
 
 
 ORDER GRANTING PETITION TO RESIGN IN GOOD STANDING 
(By: Recktenwald, C.J., McKenna and Eddins, JJ., 
and Intermediate Court of Appeals Associate Judges 
Wadsworth and Nakasone, assigned by reason of vacancies)
  Upon consideration of the petition to resign from the 
practice of law in the State of Hawai ʻi, filed by attorney 
Candice Andrea Garcia-Rodrigo  (Petitioner), pursuant to Rule 
1.10 of the Rules of the Supreme Court of the State of Hawai ʻi 
(RSCH), but to retain the paper license as a memento, as 
authorized by RSCH 1.10(b), and the declarations  submitted in 
support thereof, we conclude that Petitioner has fully complied 
with the requirements of RSCH 1.10. Therefore,   
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the petition to resign is 
granted. Petitioner may retain the paper license as a memento.  
   
 
   
        
        
       
       
       
 
   IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that the Clerk shall remove the 
name of Petitioner, attorney number 9718, from the roll of 
attorneys of the State of Hawai ʻi, effective with the filing of 
this order.  IT IS FURTHER ORDERED pursuant to RSCH 1.10(g) that 
Petitioner shall comply with the notice, declaration, and record 
requirements of RSCH 2.16(a), (b), (d) and (g). 
DATED: Honolulu, Hawaiʻi, December 27, 2023.
