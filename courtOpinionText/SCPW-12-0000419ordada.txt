ORDER DENYING PETITION FOR WRIT OF MANDAMUS
 
(By: Recktenwald, C.J., Nakayama, Acoba, and McKenna, JJ.
and Circuit Judge To 'oto'o, in place of Duffy, J., recused)
 
Upon consideration of petitioner Edmund M. Abordo's
 
petition for a writ of mandamus, the respondent judge's answer,
 
and the record of Cr. No. 93-0737, it appears that the petitions
 
for writs of habeas corpus submitted by petitioner were filed in
 
Cr. No. 93-0737 on January 24, 2012 and February 17, 2012 and
 
were disposed of on May 7, 2012. Petitioner's request for
 
mandamus relief is moot. Therefore,
 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the petition for a writ of
 
mandamus is denied as moot.
