(By: Recktenwald, C.J., Nakayama, Acoba, and McKenna, JJ.
and Circuit Judge To 'oto'o, in place of Duffy, J., recused)
 
Upon consideration of petitioner Cedric Ah Sing's
 
petition for a writ of mandamus, the respondent judge's answer,
 
and the record of Cr. No. 56581, it appears that the petition for 
writ of habeas corpus submitted by petitioner was filed in Cr.
 
No. 56581 on February 6, 2012 and was disposed of on May 7, 2012. 
Petitioner's request for mandamus relief is moot. Therefore,
 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the petition for a writ of
 
mandamus is denied as moot.
