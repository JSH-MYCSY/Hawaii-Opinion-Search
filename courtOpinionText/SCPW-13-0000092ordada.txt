By: Recktenwald, C.J., Nakayama, Acoba, McKenna, and Pollack, JJ.)
 
Upon consideration of petitioner Richard Cohan’s
 
petition for a writ of mandamus, filed on February 14, 2013,
 
respondents Marriott Hotel Services, Inc. dba Marriott’s Ko Olina
 Beach Club, Marriott Ownership Resorts, Inc. dba Marriott
 Vacation Club International, and RRB Restaurants, LLC dba Chuck’s
 Steak and Seafood’s answer to the petition, filed on April 3,
 2013, the respective supporting documents and the record, 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that this original proceeding will
 
be scheduled for oral argument. The parties will be notified by
 
the appellate clerk regarding scheduling.
 
IT IS HEREBY FURTHER ORDERED that each party shall file
 
a supplemental brief that addresses whether the stipulated
 qualified protective order and the authorizations that were
 ordered to be signed by the arbitrator comply with federal and
 state law. Each party’s supplemental brief shall not exceed 10
 pages in length and shall be filed within 14 days after the
 filing of this order.
