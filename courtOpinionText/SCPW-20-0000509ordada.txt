   IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Motion to Amend is 
granted:   
1.  Paragraph 2.a. of the August 27, 2020 “Order Re: 
Petty Misdemeanor, Misdemeanor, and Felony Defendants” (“August 
27, 2020 Order”) is amended to include all offenses in HRS 
chapter 707, which includes all sexual offenses set forth in HRS 
chapter 707, part V.  Paragraph 2.a. shall now read as follows: 
 2.  For purposes of this order, the following are 
“excluded offenses”: 
  a.  all offenses in HRS chapter 707;  
 
 The provisions of the August 27, 2020 Order remain in 
effect in all other respects. 
 
 2.  For any individual charged with an “excluded 
offense” as defined under the August 27, 2020 Order, and as 
amended herein, the trial judges retain discretion to set bail 
and conditions of release.
