  Upon consideration of the petition for writ of mandamus filed on November 30, 2022, and the record, 
Petitioner’s request for relief in 3CCV-22-0000084 is before 
Judge Henry Nakamoto, and, as such, should be addressed by the 
circuit court.  An extraordinary writ is not warranted at this 
time.  Kema v. Gaddis, 91 Hawaiʻ i 200, 204, 982 P.2d 334, 338 
(1999) (stating that a writ of mandamus will not issue unless 
the petitioner demonstrates a clear and indisputable right to 
relief and a lack of other means to obtain relief). Electronically Filed
