 Upon consideration of petitioner Jason Fletcher’s 
correspondences to the court and attachments filed August 15,
2023, and the record,  
 
  
  
 
 
 
 The court construes the August 15, 2023 filings as a motion 
for reconsideration of the order denying petition, filed June 26, 2023, and notes that these documents were mailed by Fletcher 
from a correctional facility beginning on or about July 3, 2023. 
It is hereby ordered: 1. The motion for reconsideration is denied. 
2. With respect to the sealed court filings at Docket 15, 
Docket 19, Docket 23, Docket 27, and Docket 33 the appellate clerk shall file a redacted version of these documents on the 
public record by redacting the “Personal Information” including 
the social security numbers and dates of birth in conformance 
with the definition of “Personal Information” in Rule 2.19 of 
the Hawaiʻ i Court Records Rules. The clerk shall file the 
redacted versions of Docket 15, Docket 19, Docket 23, Docket 27, 
Docket 33 on the record in this case as new filings, and Docket 
15, Docket 19, Docket 23, Docket 27, and Docket 33 shall remain 
sealed as they appear to contain confidential personal 
information, including social security numbers and dates of 
birth. Any individual may file a motion objecting to the sealing of these documents. 
To the extent Fletcher desires to file documents in 
Fletcher’s pending cases in the first circuit court, any 
documents should be mailed by Fletcher to the “First Circuit
