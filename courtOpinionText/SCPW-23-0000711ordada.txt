ORDER DENYING PETITION FOR WRIT OF MANDAMUS 
(By: Recktenwald, C.J., McKenna,  Eddins,  and Ginoza, JJ.,  
and Circuit Judge Kawamura, in place of Devens, J., recused)  
Upon consideration of the petition for writ of mandamus 
filed December 5, 2023, and the record, Petitioners have not 
demonstrated a clear and indisputable right to relief, nor a 
lack of alternative means to obtain the relief sought. An extraordinary writ is unwarranted. See Womble Bond Dickinson   
 
       
       (US) LLP v. Kim, 153 Hawaiʻ i  307, 319, 537 P.3d 1154, 1166 
(2023). 
It is ordered that the petition is denied.
