Upon consideration of Petitioner Aaron Zeeman’s Petition 
for Writ of Mandamus, filed May 1, 2026, and the record, 
Petitioner has not demonstrated a clear and indisputable right 
to relief, and the lack of other means to redress adequately the 
alleged wrong. See Womble Bond Dickinson (US) LLP v. Kim, 153         
       
       
       
 Hawaii 307, 319, 537 P.3d 1154, 1166 (2023). Moreover, 
Petitioner failed to establish that the conduct underlying the 
Petition is ministerial. See In re Disciplinary Bd. of Hawaiʻ i 
Supreme Court, 91 Hawaiʻ i 363, 368, 984 P.2d 688, 693 (1999).  
The Petition is denied.
