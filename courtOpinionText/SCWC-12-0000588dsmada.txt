Upon review of Petitioner/Defendant-Appellant Scott
Yang’s “Motion to Clarify Summary Disposition Order, Filed
March 20, 2017,” filed June 5, 2017, the Summary Disposition
Order filed March 20, 2017, and the Judgment on Appeal filed
April 24, 2017, this court lacks jurisdiction to consider the
motion.  See Hawai# i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 40.
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion is dismissed.
