ORDER DISMISSING MOTION FOR RECONSIDERATION
 
(
By: Recktenwald, C.J., Nakayama, McKenna, Pollack, and Wilson, JJ.)
 
Upon consideration of petitioner’s May 4, 2015, motion
 
for reconsideration of this court’s order rejecting application
 
for writ of certiorari, filed on April 8, 2015, and pursuant to
 
HRAP Rule 40.1(h), neither acceptance nor rejection of an
 
application for a writ of certiorari shall be subject to a motion
 
for reconsideration. Therefore,
 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion for
 
reconsideration is dismissed. 
DATED: Honolulu, Hawai' i, May 18, 2015.
