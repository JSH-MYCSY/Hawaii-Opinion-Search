of Appeals at the time the applications for certiorari were filed, 
see Hawai' i Revised Statutes § 602-59(a) (Supp. 2014); see 
also
 Hawai'i Rules of Appellate Procedure (HRAP) Rule 36(b)(1) 
(2012),

filing of the intermediate court of appeals’ judgment on appeal
 or dismissal order, unless the time for filing the application is
 extended in accordance with this rule.”).
