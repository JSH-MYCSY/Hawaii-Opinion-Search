ORDER DISMISSING MOTION FOR RECONSIDERATION
 
(By: Recktenwald, C.J., Nakayama, McKenna, and Wilson, JJ., and
Circuit Judge Ayabe in place of Pollack, J., recused)
 
It appearing that Petitioner/Petitioner-Appellant
 
Roland I. Kehano, Sr.’s document entitled “Objection of Order
 
Rejecting Application for Writ of Certiorari,” filed on November
 12, 2015, is a motion for reconsideration of this court’s
 rejection of Petitioner’s application for a writ of certiorari on
 November 2, 2015,
 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion is dismissed 
pursuant to Hawai
'i Rules of Appellate Procedure Rule 40.1(h) 
(2014) (“Neither acceptance nor rejection of an application for a writ of certiorari shall be subject to a motion for
 
reconsideration in the supreme court. The rejection of an
 application for certiorari shall be final.”).
