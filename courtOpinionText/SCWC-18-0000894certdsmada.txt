The court having given further consideration to the 
record and motions in the instant case, it appears that the writ
of certiorari herein was improvidently granted . Accordingly,  
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that this certiorari proceeding 
is dismissed.   This dismissal is without prejudice to a 
subsequent Hawai‘i Rules of Penal Procedure Rule 40 petition on
the ineffective assistance of counsel claim.
