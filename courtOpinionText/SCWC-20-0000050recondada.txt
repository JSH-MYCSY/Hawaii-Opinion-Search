ORDER DENYING MOTION FOR RECONSIDERATION
(By: Recktenwald, C.J., Nakayama, McKenna, and Wilson, JJ. )1
On July 10, 2020, petitioner/defendant-appellant
Francis A. Grandinetti filed a document entitled, “Petitioner-
Defendant-Appellant on Reconsideration of Certiorari, On Ten-Day
Review, June 25, 2020 Judgment (Panel Decision); Errors”, in
which he asks this court to reconsider the June 25, 2020 order
rejecting his application for writ of certiorari.  A rejection of
an application for writ of certiorari, however, is not subject to
reconsideration.  See HRAP Rule 40.1(h) (“Neither acceptance nor
rejection of an application for a writ of certiorari shall be
subject to a motion for reconsideration in the supreme court.
