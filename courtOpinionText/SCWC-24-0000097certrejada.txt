ORDER DISMISSING APPLICATION FOR WRIT OF CERTIORARI 
(By: Recktenwald, C.J., McKenna, Eddins, Ginoza, and Devens, JJ.)  
It appearing that the judgment on appeal in the above-
referenced matter not having been filed by the Intermediate 
Court of Appeals at the time the application for writ of certiorari was filed, see 
Hawaiʻi Revised Statutes § 602-59(a) 
(2017); see also Hawaiʻ i Rules of Appellate Procedure (HRAP) 
Rule 36(b)(1) (2016), 
It is ordered that Petitioner/Petitioner-Appellant’s 
application for writ of certiorari, filed May 9, 2025, is    
 
 dismissed without prejudice to re-filing the application 
pursuant to HRAP Rule 40.1(a) (2023) (“The application shall be filed within thirty days after the filing of the intermediate court of appeals’ judgment on appeal or dismissal order, unless the time for filing the application is extended in accordance 
with this rule.”).
