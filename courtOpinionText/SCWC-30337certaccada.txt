Petitioner/Defendant-Appellant’s application for writ 
of certiorari filed on August 24, 2012, is hereby accepted and 
will be scheduled for oral argument. The parties will be notified by the appellate clerk regarding scheduling. 
IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that each party shall file a 
supplemental brief addressing the issue of whether sufficient evidence existed to indict Petitioner under Hawai' i Revised 
Statutes § 708-830.5(1)(a) for theft “[o]f property or services, the value of which exceeds $20,000[.]” Each party’s supplemental brief shall not exceed 10 pages in length and shall be filed 
within 14 days after the filing of this order.
