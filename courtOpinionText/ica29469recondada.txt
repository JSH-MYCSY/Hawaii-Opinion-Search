Upon review of Defendant-Appellant Erin Ackerman's 
(Appellant) Motion for Reconsideration of Summary Disposition 
Order filed on November 1, 2012, pursuant to Hawai
'i Rules of 
Appellate Procedure (HRAP) Rule 40 and the record, Appellant's motion fails to demonstrate that this court overlooked or 
misapprehended any points of law or fact. See HRAP Rule 40. 
Therefore, 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion for
 
reconsideration is denied.
