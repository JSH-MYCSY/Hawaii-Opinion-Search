The Summary Disposition Order, filed on May 26, 2011,
 
is hereby corrected as follows:

that is between "reliable" and "required" should be changed to
 "were". 
The clerk of the court is directed to take all
 
necessary steps to notify the publishing agencies of these
 changes.
