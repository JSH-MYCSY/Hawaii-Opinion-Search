ORDER DISMISSING APPEAL PURSUANT TO HRAP RULE 24
(By: Nakamura, C.J., Foley and Fujise, JJ.)
Upon review of the record, it appears that: 
(1) Claimant-Appellant Vinh Alkire-Clemen (Appellant) filed a
notice of appeal on August 18, 2009; (2) Appellant did not pay
the filing fee; (3) on October 30, 2009, the appellate clerk
informed Appellant: (a) the record on appeal could not be filed
without payment of the filing fee pursuant to Rule 11(b)(2) of
the Hawai #i Rules of Appellate Procedure (HRAP) or an executed
motion to proceed in forma pauperis pursuant to HRAP Rule 24; and
(b) the matter would be called to the attention of the court on
November 6, 2009 for such action as the court deemed proper,
which may include dismissal of the appeal; and (4) Appellant
failed to pay the filing fee or submit a motion to proceed in
forma pauperis.  Therefore, 
IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the appeal is dismissed
pursuant to HRAP Rule 24.
DATED: Honolulu, Hawai# i, February 16, 2010.
