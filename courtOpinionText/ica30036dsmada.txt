(Nakamura, C.J., Fujise and Ginoza, JJ.)
 
Upon consideration of the "Motion To Dismiss Appeal
 
Filed September 3, 2009" (Motion To Dismiss Appeal) filed on
 
January 31, 2012, by Defendant-Appellee Association of Apartment
 Owners of Queen Emma Gardens (AOAO), the response and request for
 sanctions filed by Defendants-Appellees Tommy Wai Hung Ma and
 Sindy Sin Yee Ma (Ma Appellees), the Joinder in the Motion To
 Dismiss Appeal filed by Plaintiffs-Appellants, and the record and
 files in this case,
 IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that:
 
(a) The Motion To Dismiss Appeal is granted and the
 
remaining appeals by Appellants Phyllis L. Gomes, individually,
 and Tylerann Carvalho-Gomes are dismissed. By granting the
 Motion To Dismiss Appeal, this court makes no determination as to
 the validity or propriety of the releases between the parties. 
We grant the Motion To Dismiss Appeal because we are satisfied
 that the remaining parties who have appealed (Appellants Phyllis
 L. Gomes, individually, and Tylerann Carvalho-Gomes) wish to
 dismiss their appeals.
 
(b) The Ma Appellees' request for sanctions is denied.
 
(c) There being no further appeals in Appeal No. 30036,
 
this case is dismissed.
