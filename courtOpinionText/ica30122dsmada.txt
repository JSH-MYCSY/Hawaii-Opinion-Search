With Prejudice, filed October 28, 2015, by Defendant-Appellant

Rules of Appellate Procedure (HRAP) Rule 42(a); (3) because the appeal was docketed on December 15, 2009, dismissal is authorized by HRAP Rule 42(b); and (4) the parties agree to bear their own
