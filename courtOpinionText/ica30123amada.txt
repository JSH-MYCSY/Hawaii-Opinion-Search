IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the Summary Disposition Order
 
filed on January 31, 2013, is corrected as follows: 
On page 1, in footnote 1, the case citation to State v.
 
Graybeard is corrected by adding the words "Honolulu v." after
 
the words "City & Cnty. of" and before the word "Midkiff" so that
 the citation reads:
 
State v. Graybeard, 93 Hawai' i 513, 516, 6 P.3d 385, 388 (App.
2000) (internal quotation marks omitted) (City & Cnty. of Honolulu 
v. Midkiff, 57 Haw. 273, 275076, 554 P.2d 233, 235 (1976)).
 
The clerk of the court is directed to take all 
necessary steps to notify the publishing agencies of this change.
