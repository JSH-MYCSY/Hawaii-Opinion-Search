MATSUE TANIGUCHI AND ASSOCIATES, Plaintiff-Appellant, v.

APPEAL FROM THE DISTRICT COURT OF THE THIRD CIRCUIT
(CIVIL NO. 3RC09-1-0308K)
ORDER DISMISSING APPEAL PURSUANT TO HRAP RULE 30
(By: Nakamura, C.J., Foley and Leonard, JJ.)
Upon review of the record, it appears that: 
(1) Plaintiff-Appellant Matsue Taniguchi and Associates
(Appellant) filed a notice of appeal on October 23, 2009; (2) on
December 22, 2009,  the appellate clerk filed a notice of
entering case on calendar and notified Appellant the
jurisdictional statement was due on January 1, 2010 and the
opening brief was due on January 31, 2010; (3) Appellant did not
file the jurisdictional statement or the opening brief; (4) on
February 4, 2010, the appellate clerk informed Appellant that 
(a) the time to file the jurisdictional statement and the opening
brief expired; (b) the matter would be brought to the attention
of the court on February 11, 2010 for such action as the court
deems proper; and (c) the appeal may be dismissed pursuant to
Hawai#i Rules of Appellate Procedure (HRAP) Rule 30; (5) although
Appellant wrote to the appellate clerk acknowledging receipt of
the letter, Appellant did not file the jurisdictional statement
or the opening brief.  Therefore,NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI #I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
-2-IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the appeal is dismissed
pursuant to HRAP Rule 30.
DATED: Honolulu, Hawai# i, April 22, 2010.
