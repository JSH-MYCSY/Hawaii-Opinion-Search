Upon review of the record, it appears that: 
(1) Defendant-Appellant Alfred Napahuelua Spinney
(Appellant) filed a notice of appeal on October 23, 2009;
(2) on April 1, 2010, the appellate clerk filed the
record on appeal and informed Appellant that the jurisdictional
statement was due on April 11, 2010, and the opening brief was
due on May 11, 2010; 
(3) Appellant did not file either document; 
(4) on June 17, 2010, the appellate clerk informed
Appellant that: (a) the time to file the jurisdictional statement
and opening brief had expired, (b) the matter would be called to
the attention of the court on June 24, 2010, and (c) the appeal
may be dismissed, and
(5) Appellant did not file the required documents nor
request relief from default.  Therefore,NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI #I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
