(Appellee Sakamaki) and Donna J. Walden's (Appellee Walden)

to Rule 40 of the Hawai# i Rules of Appellate Procedure (HRAP),
and (3) the record, it appears that the intermediate court of
appeals did not overlook or misapprehend any points of law or
fact when the intermediate court of appeals entered the May 28,

reconsideration of the May 28, 2010 dismissal order is denied.
