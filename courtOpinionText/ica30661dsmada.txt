(By: Nakamura, C.J., Leonard and Ginoza, JJ.)
 
Upon consideration of Petitioner-Appellee Denise
 
Houghtaling’s Motion to Dismiss Appeal, the papers in support,
 
and the records and files herein, it appears that: (1) On
 
August 17, 2010, the appellate clerk filed the record on appeal
 
and notified Respondent-Appellant Peter Frost (Appellant) that
 
the statement of jurisdiction was due August 30, 2010 and the
 
opening brief was due September 25, 2010; (2) Appellant filed the
 
statement of jurisdiction on August 30, 2010; (3) the court
 
granted Appellant two extensions of time to file the opening
 
brief, but Appellant did not file the opening brief; (4) on
 
December 28, 2010, Appellee moved to dismiss this appeal due to
 
Appellant’s failure to file the opening brief; and (5) Appellant
 
did not respond to or oppose the motion to dismiss. 
NOT FOR PUBLICATION IN WEST'S HAWAI 'I REPORTS AND PACIFIC REPORTER
 
Therefore, IT IS HEREBY ORDERED that the motion to
 
dismiss is granted, and this appeal is dismissed.
